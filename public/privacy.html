Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Rainy is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our weather application.

2. Data Collection

We may collect non-personal data such as device type, browser, and location (with your permission) to provide accurate weather forecasts. We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you contact us directly.

3. Data Usage

Collected data is used solely to improve app functionality, deliver weather information, and enhance user experience. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Rainy may use third-party APIs (e.g., weather data providers, map services) that have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing those policies for more information.

5. Cookies

Rainy may use cookies or similar technologies to remember your preferences and improve performance. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us if you wish to exercise these rights.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at me@syslink.dev.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
